Mariah Carey Season

An Interactive Visualization of the Top Christmas Songs

Christmas is coming, and we're starting to listen to holiday music!

Each bubble represents a song on the Billboard Chart, color-coded by the number of weeks spent on it and sized according to peak position reached. Let's group the songs by the number of weeks, instance and decade on the chart to see which have been most popular.

First, we look at the top songs on the Billboard chart from 1958 through 2017. Since some songs appeared on the chart multiple times, they have more than one bubble.

Now, click on the 'By Weeks on Chart' button to see how many weeks the top songs spent on the chart.
